Living with a GT3 RS - a beginning

December 13, 2023 by daveo4porsche

After a year of searching, I've finally become the proud owner of a 2023 Porsche 911 GT3 RS, officially taking possession on December 8th. I wanted to create this thread to share my early impressions of ownership rather than focus solely on the acquisition process. I opted for the Weissach Package, as it balances performance with practicality for both track days and street driving. While I’m concerned about the magnesium wheels' durability, I appreciate the added features that come with the package. My history with Porsche spans multiple models, giving me a solid foundation for evaluating this car. In the few days since picking it up, I’ve found it to be a quintessential 911 GT—noticeably present on the road, with acceptable ride and road noise levels. The car truly shines at higher speeds, where its superior aerodynamics come into play. Build quality has been impressive, and the updated PCM system, with wireless CarPlay and enhanced digital displays, significantly enhances the driving experience. The steering feels precise, and the car handles beautifully, almost as if it’s on rails. I do miss certain features from previous models, like the heated steering wheel and more intuitive parking camera, but the overall performance and engagement make up for it. The GT3 RS draws attention wherever it goes, and I can’t wait to take it on the track to explore its full potential.

I would love to own a Porsche GT3 RS one day, and I can already picture the perfect Monday mornings spent with my wife. Each week, we’d set off early, the sun just starting to rise, illuminating the winding roads ahead of us. The thrill of the drive would be amplified by the stunning scenery of the canyons, the curves beckoning us to push the limits while embracing the car's incredible handling. I can imagine us sharing laughter and conversations, the sound of the engine harmonizing with our excitement as we navigate the turns. These drives wouldn’t just be about the destination; they’d be a ritual that brings us closer together, an escape from the everyday hustle, and a way to start our week on a high note. With the GT3 RS, every journey would be an unforgettable adventure, creating memories that we’d cherish for a lifetime.

Porsche 992 GT3RS Track Suspension Setup

by Suspension Secrets

In April, we attended a BRDC track day at Silverstone to refine the setup for the 992-generation Porsche 911 GT3 RS, collaborating with Leyton Clarke of RMA Track Days. Silverstone's diverse corners made it an ideal location for developing a versatile chassis. Leyton's GT3 RS featured the Weissach Package and Suspension Secrets Front Camber Blocks, which we discussed with other drivers using similar setups. During our testing, we identified inadequate front axle camber as a significant issue, particularly with high-performance Michelin tyres. The Suspension Secrets Camber Blocks provide an effective solution for increasing camber without affecting caster angle, crucial for handling the GT3 RS’s increased downforce. Leyton's initial laps revealed mid-corner understeer, prompting us to adjust camber and introduce toe out for better turn-in response. We also discovered that issues with the active DRS systems stemmed from an unannounced software update by Porsche. After refining the alignment and geometry, we focused on the electronically-controlled differential and dampers. Adjustments to the differential settings helped alleviate understeer, while damper tuning improved stability on Silverstone's smooth tarmac. By the end of the day, we achieved a well-balanced setup, enhancing the car's stability and performance.

The Porsche 911 GT3 RS is an extraordinary testament to automotive engineering, seamlessly blending cutting-edge technology, precision handling, and raw power to create a driving experience that’s nothing short of exhilarating.
